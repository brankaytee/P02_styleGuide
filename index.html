About Us

BAR CANDY® creator Liz Green was a bartender in Los Angeles and started experimenting with flavors behind the bar. Before too long she was winning cocktail competitions and liquor companies were hiring her to create cocktails for their brands.She then created menus for restaurants, bars and nightclubs, and authored an original cocktail book called Cocktail Hollywood.

She ran a series of nightclubs in New York, and after realizing that the cocktail craze wasn’t going anywhere, she decided to create an experience. It was at this time that Bar Candy® was born!

FUN FACT: BAR CANDY® made it to the final round of Shark Tank beating out thousands of people. It came down to BAR CANDY® and another brand!

FAQ

Do the glasses (cones) hold drinks and champagne for a long time?

Yes. The glasses can hold liquids and desserts for several hours at room temperature. Please do not use in the heat and and hot weather outside.

Can I store them?

Yes. They can be stored at room temperature for 24-48 hours. After this they should be refrigerated or frozen. They can last for weeks and months when frozen. Please try and use at room temperature as this is when the chocolate is the most fresh. We do not add preservatives.

Are they Kosher?

Yes. Kosher OU

How high is the quality of chocolate?

The chocolate is the highest quality Belgian chocolate. No additives. Natural ingredients with a high cocoa butter percentage for a shiny stunning visual. The chocolate is also very fragrant and will fill the are with a delightful aroma

Do you ship over the summer months?

Yes. We ship overnight

Do you ship internationally?

Yes.

I own a restaurant and nightclub. Can I use these with my own glassware?

Yes. They will fit into most wine glasses. We do recommend you use ours as it is a custom fit. Simply purchase the glass holders once and re use.

I am ordering for a wedding. Can I have these shipped straight to the hotel/space?

Yes. We will ship right to the venue.

I may not be home when these arrive. Can I specify a time?

We usually do overnight shipping. For an extra fee, they can be delivered early in the morning. We prefer if you are home, as they could melt outside.

How do you ship the gift items?

We usually ship 2-3 day delivery. Overnight is also available.

How much lead time is required?

We like to have 7-10 day lead time. We can do a rush for an extra fee.

Can I mix and match the colors and flavors?

Yes. You can have up to 3 selections per case (48 case count)
